Who are we?

Daylight Café was founded in 2019 in Cluj-Napoca, Romania. It is currently the only café in the city that also has a workspace for students or anyone working on their laptops. If you're in the city and you're looking for a cup of coffee or a delicious dessert at a good price, Daylight Café is the place for you!
